Why you should go vegan

Help to save animals: Every vegan saves more than 100 animals a year! There is simply no easier way to help animals and prevent suffering.

Save the planet: Consuming meat is actually one of the worst things that you can do for the Earth. It is wasteful and causes enormous amounts of pollution.

Help feed the world: Eating meat takes tons of crops and water to raise farmed animals. All that plant food could be used much more efficiently if it were fed directly to people.

Vegan diet is great for your health: According to the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics, vegans are less likely to develop heart disease, cancer, diabetes, or high blood pressure than meat-eaters are.
